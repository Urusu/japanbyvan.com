Please note

All drivers are required to present his/her drivers license. The following licenses are valid in Japan to drive a rented vehicle:

  1. Japanese drivers license
  2. International driving permit under Convention of Road Traffic of 1949.
  3. Japanese translation issued by an authorized organization for licenses issued in Switzerland, Germany, France, Belgium, Taiwan, Slovenia or Monaco as well as original driving license issued in these countries/area.

Details about Drivers License

  • We will ask for a damage deposit of JPY100,000 due to insurance restrictions. This is the max. amount youd have to pay in case of a self-inflicted accident. Read more
  • Bring your original drivers license. Please note that we cant accept copies or photos.
